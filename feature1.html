Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q: What delivery days are there in my area?

A: We offer delivery across all 7 days in a week.

Q: How does my food stay fresh in transit?

A: We have specially designed boxes with insulated liners and cooling gel packs that are modified seasonally to ensure optimal freshness in transit. Once you receive your box, please make sure to unpack the ingredients and refrigerate all items immediately.

Q: Where do we deliver?

A: We deliver all across Bhopal And Indore cities.

Q: What are the delivery timings?

A: We deliver all 7 days a week from 8am to 8pm.

Q: What if I am not at home to accept the delivery?

A: No problem! We'll drop the box at your doorstep, so it will be patiently waiting for you when you get home. No signatures necessary.

Q: When will my order be delivered?

A: The orders usually arrive at the same day it is placed or it may be delivered next day if it is late or heavy hours.

Q: What is the delivery charge for an order on Boxfresh?

A: For any order value below Rs. 100, a delivery fee of Rs. 25 will be applied. All orders above Rs. 100 will not incur any delivery charge.

Q: Will someone inform me if my order delivery gets delayed?

A: In case of a delay, our customer support team will keep you updated about your delivery.

Q: What is the minimum order value required for delivery?

A: There is no minimum order value needed for delivery.
