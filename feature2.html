Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q: What payment methods does Boxfresh accept?

A: We currently accept all major credit and debit cards (VISA, MasterCard, American Express, RuPay), netbanking, UPIs, cash as well as e-rupee as a payment method.

Q: How does payment work? Is it secure?

A: We take payment security very seriously and do not store our customers' full credit card information on file. Instead, we bill using a secure method called tokenization. A token is an encrypted, randomly-generated code that is assigned to your payment details during the checkout to securely bill you.

Q: What happens if my payment fails?

A: If your payment fails during checkout, the amount, if debited, will be transferred back into the original payment form within 2 working days.

Q: Why did my payment fail?

A: Your payment can fail if you entered incorrect information, improper internet connection, insufficient funds in account or any other reason.

Q: How do I view invoices?

A: Your order invoice will be sent to your registered email address after the order is delivered. You can view your invoices there.

Q: Are there any other charges or taxes in addition to the price shown?

A: There are no other charges accept the delivery charge if the order is under Rs. 100. However, GST applicable as per Government Regulizations is already included in the price shown.

Q: What is the meaning of cash on delivery?

A: Cash on delivery means that you can pay for your order at the time of order delivery at your doorstep.
